Per Diem Coverage & Pass-Through % — Controls how much of the GSA per diem rate is passed through to the candidate
%
Example — Standard CONUS ($178/day, 5 days/wk, 40 hrs/wk):
Full GSA per diem: $178.00/day → $22.25/hr
At 100%: Effective per diem = $178.00/day → $22.25/hr  |  Withheld (taxable): $0.00/hr
100% — Full GSA rate passed to candidate (tax-free, non-taxable stipend)  ·  Partial % — Only that share is passed; the remainder stays in your cost/margin pool  ·  0% — No per diem applied even if location has a GSA rate
